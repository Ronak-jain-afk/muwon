# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Music listeners who want to discover, organize, and stream high-quality music on desktop and mobile. Users are often multitasking, working, or relaxing, and need a low-friction way to start music or browse curated content.

## Product Purpose
A music streaming web player that allows users to stream songs, watch music videos, and manage their music library. Success is defined by high immersion, effortless playback control, and engaging discovery mechanics.

## Brand Personality
Immersive, Energetic, Restrained. The UI is dark and recedes, allowing colorful album art and visual media to take center stage.

## Anti-references
- **Standard Admin Dashboards**: Avoid typical gray backgrounds, heavy borders, data tables, and high-density information layouts.
- **SaaS Branding**: No bright neon gradients, "glassmorphism" used purely as decoration, or light-mode interfaces by default.
- **Cluttered Visuals**: Avoid crowded card layouts with double borders, tiny tracked uppercase eyebrows on every single element, or sketchy SVG placeholders.

## Design Principles
1. **Artwork-First Presence**: The interface acts as a canvas; the colors, vibe, and focus are driven by the album art.
2. **Immersive Ambient Glow**: Backgrounds dynamically reflect the mood and palette of the playing media through subtle, performance-conscious blur overlays.
3. **Muscle-Memory Control**: Playback controls (play, pause, skip, volume, progress bar) must remain predictable, highly readable, and positioned consistently to respect user habits.
4. **Fluid Horizontal Curation**: Use horizontal carousels and smooth swipe/scroll rhythms for browsing, keeping the visual hierarchy simple and focused.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **WCAG 2.1 AA Contrast**: High-contrast text (#FFFFFF or high-lightness grays) against the dark interface.
- **Media Player Hotkeys**: Native support for keyboard navigation (Space, Arrow keys, M for mute).
- **Reduced Motion**: Respect `prefers-reduced-motion` for transitions, ambient glows, and carousel sliding animations.
